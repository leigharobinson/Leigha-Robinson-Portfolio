About Me.

I’m a former elite women’s ice hockey athlete with a diverse background in biochemistry studies and a BA in Music. The dynamic path that I've taken to become a Software developer helped sharpen my aptitude for working in team environments, further developed my exceptional communication and collaboration skills, and granted me a unique perspective when problem-solving. Most importantly, my journey gave me lots of experience learning and mastering new skill sets and fine-tuning the process of achievement.

During my time at Nashville Software School, I became proficient at building applications incorporating front-end languages. I also gained invaluable experience working in remote teams and implementing external APIs, React, and JQuery. In addition, I’ve leveraged my design skills with FIGMA for wire-framing and mock-ups as well as creating in app design components.

As a Junior Developer, the skills I’ve built over the years and the challenges that come with programming have allowed me to grow in new and exciting ways. I'm inspired by the invaluable ways programming can be applied to solve problems in our everyday life and look forward to developing my skills further in this field. I'm particularly fascinated by UI/UX design and how it creates a meaningful user experience.

Front-End Capstone

Forget Me Not

Gift Cards, Coupons, & Loyalty Rewards Tracker

I created an app that allows users to build a virtual wallet that can fast sort their Gift Cards, Coupons and Rewards as well as track important information such as expiration dates.

I like to be smart with my money and hate forgetting to use the various of Gift Cards, Coupons, & Loyalty Rewards in my wallet. One of the big obstacles I have to using my cards is that there are so many it's hard to remember what I have. I also regularly forget other important details such as when they expire or what I intended to use them for. This App is a way to help organize all this information so that users can quickly know what they have available for use.
